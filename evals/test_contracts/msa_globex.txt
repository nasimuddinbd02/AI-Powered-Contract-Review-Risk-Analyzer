MASTER SERVICES AGREEMENT

This Master Services Agreement ("Agreement") is made by and between Acme
Corporation and Globex LLC ("Vendor"), effective as of January 1, 2026.

1. PAYMENT TERMS
Customer shall pay all invoices net 30 days. Late payments shall accrue a
penalty and late fee of 1.5% per month on any overdue amount.

2. LIMITATION OF LIABILITY
In no event shall Vendor's aggregate liability exceed the fees paid in the prior
twelve months. Notwithstanding the foregoing, Customer accepts unlimited
liability for any breach, without limitation.

3. INDEMNIFICATION
Customer shall indemnify and hold harmless Vendor from any and all claims in
Vendor's sole discretion.

4. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY
All work product and intellectual property created under this Agreement shall be
owned exclusively by Vendor, who assigns all right, title, and interest.

5. AUTO-RENEWAL
This Agreement shall automatically renew for successive one-year renewal terms
unless terminated at least 60 days prior to the renewal date.

6. GOVERNING LAW
This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of the State of Delaware.
