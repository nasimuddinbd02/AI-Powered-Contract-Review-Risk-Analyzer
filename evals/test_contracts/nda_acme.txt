MUTUAL NON-DISCLOSURE AGREEMENT

This Mutual Non-Disclosure Agreement ("Agreement") is made by and between Acme
Corporation and Initech Inc ("Recipient"), effective March 1, 2026.

1. CONFIDENTIALITY
Each party shall hold the other party's Confidential Information in strict
confidence and shall not disclose it to any third party. This obligation
survives termination for a period of three years.

2. TERMINATION
Either party may terminate this Agreement upon 30 days written notice. Upon
termination, each party shall return or destroy all Confidential Information.

3. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY
No license to any intellectual property is granted under this Agreement. All
pre-existing intellectual property remains owned by its originating party.

4. GOVERNING LAW
This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of the State of California.

5. DISPUTE RESOLUTION
Any dispute shall first be subject to good-faith negotiation, then binding
arbitration administered in San Francisco, California.
